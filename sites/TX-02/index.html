The Platform

How It Works

SEVA manages volunteer events with scheduling, sign-ins, attendance verification, hours tracking, reviews, and analytics. The platform uses Google Apps Script and Google Sheets as its backend, with Calendar APIs for event management and scheduling automation.

Attendance verification relies on geolocation via the Haversine formula and Google Geocoding API, combined with QR and code-based check-ins for flexible sign-in options. The Gemini API powers AI analytics, delivering insights on volunteer engagement and helping nonprofits make data-driven decisions about their programs.
