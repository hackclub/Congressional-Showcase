About Me

I'm Dhruv Singh, a junior at Westview High School in Portland, Oregon. I have extensive robotics experience and co-founded Gearforward, a Portland-based 501(c)(3) focused on STEM education for underserved students. I also coach a FIRST Tech Robotics team.

The App

What ReinforceLearn Does

ReinforceLearn is a comprehensive educational platform teaching reinforcement learning fundamentals through hands-on resources. The application features seven main sections covering RL overview, algorithm types, a learning hub, pathway information, a playground environment, robotics simulations, and more.

Users can explore GitHub projects, research headlines, internship opportunities, and additional resources, all designed to make reinforcement learning accessible to beginners.
