My Story

From family frustration to a national solution

TechMate wasn’t born in a classroom. It came from years of watching the people I love most become targets simply because technology left them behind.

01
The Spark — A Family Scammed
My grandparents were repeatedly scammed online. Fake tech support calls, phishing emails, fraudulent links — they couldn’t distinguish real from dangerous. As their go-to tech helper, I realized I couldn’t be there for every senior in America.
02
Taking Action — Senior Center Visits
Before writing a single line of code, I took my ideas directly to the community. I hosted technology education presentations at senior living facilities across Las Vegas, learning firsthand what older adults struggled with most.
03
Building the Curriculum
The community response was overwhelming. I used what I heard to build a plain-language AI curriculum — zero jargon, zero condescension — designed from the ground up for older adults encountering digital technology for the first time.
04
TechMate Is Born
I expanded those presentations into a full AI-powered platform. TechMate answers technology questions in plain language, catches scams before they cause damage, explains confusing screens from photos, and teaches seniors how AI actually works.
05
Capitol Hill — National Winner
TechMate was named a 2025 Congressional App Challenge National Winner. I am honored to present it here at the Capitol — and deeply grateful to the seniors of Las Vegas whose stories made it real. The work is just beginning.
What TechMate Does

Five tools.
One mission.

💬
Core Feature
Ask Anything
Type or speak any technology question. TechMate asks clarifying questions, then walks you through the answer step by step in plain, friendly language — no jargon, ever.
Why does my laptop run slow?
There are a few common reasons. Let me ask: how old is the laptop and is it plugged in?

Got it! Here are 3 quick fixes to speed it up…
📷
Visual Help
Screenshot Helper
Upload a photo of any confusing screen. TechMate analyzes it and explains exactly what you’re looking at — and what to do next.
🛡
Safety
Scam Checker
Paste a suspicious text, email, or link. TechMate scans for fraud patterns, assigns a risk level, and tells you exactly what to watch for.
🎓
Education
Learn About AI
A structured 6-unit curriculum covering what AI is, what it can and can’t do, and how to stay safe. Built directly from senior community feedback.
🤖
Practice Space
AI Chat — Zero Pressure
A friendly conversational AI for seniors to get comfortable with technology at their own pace. Trivia, recipes, jokes, and general conversation in a warm, welcoming environment.
