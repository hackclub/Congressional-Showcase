The Platform

How It Works

CareLoop is an all-in-one iOS platform built to prevent caregiver burnout by streamlining schedules and medication regimens into a single, intuitive interface. The app provides a dual-role system with separate experiences for patients and caregivers, connected through a bidirectional system using secure connection codes for real-time task synchronization.

Beyond scheduling, CareLoop offers curated professional support resources, financial guidance, and live-location tracking to keep caregivers and those they care for constantly connected. Built with Swift and UIKit, the platform leverages iOS Calendar and Location Services to deliver a native, responsive experience that puts caregiving coordination at your fingertips.
