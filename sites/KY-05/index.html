The App

How It Works

SafeLink is an emergency management app designed specifically for rural Appalachian communities that are often underserved during natural disasters. The app features an emergency dashboard with one-tap access to safe check-ins and help requests for food, medicine, or rescue. A panic alert sends instant notifications when seconds matter most.

The live map provides a real-time view of nearby requests, safe users, and shelter locations, giving both residents and responders full situational awareness. Two-way responder messaging keeps the lines of communication open even when traditional networks are strained. The entire app is built with a rural-ready design that prioritizes low-bandwidth performance and simplicity.
