Ripple Effect
Play the Game. Change the World.
A gamified kindness platform that turns everyday actions into impact. Like a pebble in water—one action creates expanding waves of change.

About the Creator

Emma Amend — OH-08 (Warren Davidson)

Bishop Fenwick High School • 10th Grade • Liberty Township, Ohio


First-year Congressional App Challenge participant focused on gamification and social impact to make kindness more engaging and rewarding.

Gamification Social Impact Mental Health

Inspiration

“I was inspired by a disconnect in the people around me. While we’re more digitally connected than ever, loneliness and mental health struggles continue to rise among teens. I wanted to turn kindness into something engaging using game mechanics like points, streaks, and rewards. The ripple effect symbolizes how one small action can create meaningful change.”
