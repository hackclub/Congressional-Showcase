The App

What Chronos Does

Chronos is a calendar coordination SaaS platform that synchronizes the Google Calendars of a group of people. It automatically identifies available meeting times when all participants are free, then schedules the meeting with a single click.

No more back-and-forth "when are you free?" messages. Chronos finds the answer instantly.
