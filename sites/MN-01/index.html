DERMASIGHT

A skin analysis app that helps you spot potential concerns early, before they become something to worry about. Point, scan, and get a clear result in seconds.

6 Step analysis pipeline
3 Risk levels: low, moderate, high
4+ Accessibility features
01 THE BUILDER

I'm Simon Wilke, a student developer building AI tools that focus on real-world impact. I believe technology should serve people, not the other way around.

DermaSight combines on-device Core ML inference with an optional cloud AI ensemble, fusing multiple model outputs into a single explainable risk score. It is designed to assist, not diagnose, and always encourages professional medical follow-up.

02 HOW IT WORKS
1

Capture & Check

The image is validated, normalized, and scored for quality before any analysis runs. Non-skin photos are rejected immediately.

2

Analyze

A Core ML model runs on-device. Rule-based ABCDE dermatology features are computed. An optional cloud AI ensemble adds a second opinion.

3

Results

All signals are fused into a single risk score (low, moderate, or high) with a confidence rating and plain-language recommendations.

03 UNDER THE HOOD

Architecture

DermaSight runs analysis directly on your device using Apple's built-in machine learning tools. No internet is required for a result. When enabled, it can also consult cloud AI models for a second opinion and combines both perspectives into a single score.

Every stage is built to fail gracefully. If one part of the pipeline can't run, the next best option takes over automatically.

Design Intent

Skin concerns can be stressful, and waiting to see a doctor even more so. DermaSight is built for that in-between moment, giving you something informed to go on without overstating what an app can know.

Results are always honest about their confidence, and every output ends the same way: a clear nudge to follow up with a professional.
