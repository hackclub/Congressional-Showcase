The App

How It Works

Piggy Pal combines a powerful tax calculator with intuitive expense tracking to give users a complete picture of their finances. Users start by entering their gross income, marital status, deductions, and tax credits — the app then calculates their accurate after-tax salary.

From there, the app organizes income into 7 research-backed spending categories, each with its own monthly budget. As users log expenses throughout the month, visual progress bars show how their spending stacks up against each category’s limit, making it easy to stay on track and build healthier financial habits.
