The Platform

How It Works

ElectConnect is a comprehensive civic engagement app that helps users understand elections, verify political information, and connect with their representatives. The app starts with a personalized quiz that tailors news content to the user's interests while providing bias evaluation to ensure a balanced perspective.

At its core, the app uses NLP models for AI-powered fact checking — detecting claims in political content and comparing them against a verified source database to provide accurate, unbiased analysis. Users can also access voting information including registration details, important dates, and polling locations.

The representative finder uses location-based APIs to help users discover and contact their representatives at all levels of government — local, state, and federal. By combining personalized news, fact checking, and civic connection tools, ElectConnect empowers users to become informed and active participants in democracy.
