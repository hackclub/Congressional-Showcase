About Me

I am a highschool junior in Egg Harbor Township, New Jersey. From a young age, I took an extreme liking to computer science and the brain. I realized that giving the power of being remembered to those who feel forgotten matters so much more. This is exactly what got me into computational neurology!

I absolutely love to program for minorities, but I also enjoy writing poetry, where I am regionally and nationally recognized. I hope to continue advocating for the underrepresented voices in the world through my art, writing, and programming!

BetterVeteran

BetterVeteran is a no-barrier medical consultation assistant designed to support New Jersey veterans suffering from TBIs, Intracranial hemorrhages, and PTSD. Inspired by a late grandfather, I built this to bridge the gap in neuro-tech accessibility.
