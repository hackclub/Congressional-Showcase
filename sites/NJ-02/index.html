BetterVeteran

Computational Neurology & Accessibility for NJ Veterans.

The Mission

I am a high school junior in Egg Harbor Township, New Jersey. From a young age, I took an extreme liking to computer science and the brain. I realized that giving the power of being remembered to those who feel forgotten matters so much more.

I absolutely love to program for minorities, but I also enjoy writing poetry, where I am nationally recognized. I hope to continue advocating for underrepresented voices through art and code.

The Project

BetterVeteran is a no-barrier medical consultation assistant designed to support New Jersey veterans suffering from TBIs, Intracranial hemorrhages, and PTSD. Inspired by my late grandfather, I built this to bridge the gap in neuro-tech accessibility.
