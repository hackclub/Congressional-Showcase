Neurology AI-Powered NJ-02

BetterVeteran

A no-barrier medical consultation assistant designed to support veterans suffering from Traumatic Brain Injuries and PTSD.

Creator: Uchenna Uduma
School: Egg Harbor Township HS
The Mission

How It Works

BetterVeteran leverages neuro-tech accessibility to provide instant, no-signup-required assistance. Inspired by my late grandfather’s service, I built this to ensure underrepresented groups have access to life-saving neurological screening.

The system utilizes a Gradio-powered AI classification model trained on 12,000 open-dataset images to detect intracranial hemorrhages from CT scans with high confidence levels.
