01 The Problem

Students are drowning in distractions

In today's world, staying focused is harder than ever. Between social media, YouTube, and constant notifications, students lose hours of productive time daily — and most existing tools just block websites without addressing the root habit.

We built FocusAI because we've lived this problem. We're students who needed something better.

02 The App

What FocusAI does

FocusAI is a productivity application that combines intelligent website blocking with intentional task management — helping students stay accountable, organized, and actually get things done.

01

Smart blocking

A Chrome extension blocks distracting sites during focus sessions, keeping you in the work you've committed to.

02

Task management

Organize exactly what needs to get done before you start a session — so you always know what you're working toward.

03

Habit building

Track focus streaks and build lasting discipline over time, not just one-off bursts of motivation.
