The App

How It Works

Food Fight displays daily school menus along with peer and parent reviews of each menu item, giving the entire school community a voice in cafeteria dining. Users can rate meals and read feedback from others to know what to expect each day.

The app also provides allergen information based on user preferences set during registration, ensuring students with dietary restrictions can quickly identify safe options. Built with Android Studio and Java, Food Fight brings transparency and safety to school nutrition.
