The Platform

How It Works

NationVitals is an interactive data platform that integrates public health, mental health, and socioeconomic datasets into county-level visualizations. Users can explore trends, compare regions, and uncover patterns behind issues like overdose rates and mental health outcomes.

The platform transforms complex data into clear, actionable insights showing how poverty, demographics, and access to care influence community health. By bringing together multiple data sources into a single, navigable atlas, NationVitals empowers residents, researchers, and policymakers to make informed decisions about the challenges facing their communities.
