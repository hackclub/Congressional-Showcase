The Tool

How It Works

Bias Lite is an AI-powered web application that analyzes text for biased, exclusionary, or non-inclusive language. Users paste or type their text into the interface, and the tool instantly highlights problematic phrases — from gendered terms and ableist language to coded words that may discourage diverse applicants.

For each flagged phrase, Bias Lite provides context-aware inclusive alternatives powered by natural language processing. The Flask-based backend processes text through NLP pipelines to identify bias patterns, while the frontend delivers a clean, accessible interface for real-time feedback. The tool is designed for job descriptions, resumes, and everyday writing where inclusive language makes a tangible difference.
