The App

How It Works

GreenAI provides tools to reduce your carbon footprint across three key areas: appliances, food, and transportation. Rather than demanding drastic lifestyle changes, it offers lower-effort steps towards sustainability that anyone can adopt.

At its core, GreenAI provides a free, daily-updating Carbon Intensity forecast with advice about "greener hours" and when to use energy based on trends. The app makes previously inaccessible data user-friendly and digestible, so households can make informed decisions about their energy usage.

An Alexa Skill is currently under development that will enable smart appliance scheduling based on the carbon intensity forecast, automating the process of shifting energy use to cleaner times of day.
