About Me

I'm Matthew Yang, a junior at Centennial High School in Ellicott City, Maryland. I worked with my mentor Dr. Yajie Liang at the University of Maryland School of Medicine to build CellposeCellCounter.

The App

What It Does

CellposeCellCounter leverages deep learning segmentation models to automate cell counting from microscopy images. Instead of relying on expensive commercial cell counters or tedious manual counting methods, researchers can get accurate cell count, confluency, and viability assessments in seconds.
