About NeuroLens

The Problem: Brain tumor misdiagnosis leads to treatment delays, unnecessary procedures, and over $700,000 in costs per patient. Traditional diagnostic workflows require 2-4 hours of expert radiologist time per scan. With over 30,000 new brain tumors diagnosed annually in the US, delays in diagnosis and treatment planning directly impact patient survival rates and quality of life.

The Solution: NeuroLens combines deep learning with clinical expertise to deliver:

  • Multi-task AI: simultaneous detection, classification, and segmentation
  • Clinical-grade accuracy: 96% classification, 98% detection, 0.87 avg IoU
  • Lightning-fast processing: 45 seconds vs 2-4 hours
  • Accessible design: works on mobile, tablets, and desktops
  • Real-time 3D visualization for surgical planning

Real Clinical Cases: NeuroLens has been validated on three major tumor types with verified accuracy metrics:

  • Glioma detection: 97.3% accuracy, 0.89 IoU (18.4 cm³ tumor)
  • Meningioma classification: 94.8% accuracy, 0.87 IoU (12.7 cm³ tumor)
  • Pituitary tumor segmentation: 98.2% accuracy, 0.85 IoU (9.2 cm³ tumor)
