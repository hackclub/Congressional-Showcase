The Platform

How It Works

StayReady combines hazard data from trusted sources with dynamic risk scoring for earthquakes, wildfires, and flooding. The platform provides personalized guidance and actionable checklists tailored to each user's location and specific risk profile.

The app features an interactive map with hazard overlays, location-based analysis, and AI-generated summaries drawn from official planning documents. By translating complex risk data into clear, actionable steps, StayReady empowers individuals and communities to understand their vulnerabilities and take meaningful steps toward disaster preparedness.
