BoostT1D

by Aaron Prager

Turning complex diabetes data into clear, personalized guidance

About Me

I'm Aaron, a high school student from Newton, MA, developer, and someone who has lived with Type 1 Diabetes since I was 17 months old. Managing diabetes has been part of my daily life for as long as I can remember, and over time I became deeply interested in using technology to solve the challenges that come with it.

BoostT1D brings together my personal experience with diabetes and my passion for computer science to help patients and families better understand their data, feel less overwhelmed, and make more confident daily decisions.

The Problem

Managing Type 1 Diabetes is a full-time job.

Continuous glucose monitors and diabetes devices generate large amounts of data, but interpreting that data is difficult. Patients must constantly make decisions about insulin, food, and activity, often without clear real-time guidance.

For newly diagnosed families and people without regular access to specialists, the burden can be overwhelming.

The Solution

BoostT1D is a multimodal AI platform that transforms diabetes data into meaningful, personalized insights. Instead of showing users raw numbers alone, it helps interpret patterns and provides clearer guidance— from everyday logging to community connection.

Diabetes Profile
Keep your diabetes context in one place: diagnosis and device details, goals, and preferences. That foundation helps the app tailor insights and keep recommendations relevant to how you manage T1D.
Glucose Reading
Log and review glucose values alongside CGM data when you want a manual check or a fuller picture of blood sugar over time—so decisions are based on complete information, not scattered numbers.
Carb Estimator
Use AI and computer vision to estimate carbohydrates from food photos when labels are not handy, so mealtime insulin decisions can be quicker and less guesswork-heavy.
Therapy Adjustments
Explore how patterns in your data relate to therapy choices and trends, with clearer framing for discussions with your care team. Medical decisions always belong with you and your clinician.
Find Your T1D Buddy
Match with peers who understand daily life with Type 1 Diabetes—so newly diagnosed users and families can learn from people who have already walked the path.
Find Support
Reach resources, encouragement, and practical help when diabetes feels heavy, whether you need tips, reassurance, or a path to the right kind of assistance.
Volunteer
Experienced community members can step up as mentors and supporters, offering friendship and guidance to others navigating T1D and strengthening the network for everyone.

Technology

BoostT1D combines multiple technologies into one platform:

React and TypeScript for the web app, Swift and SwiftUI for iOS, a Node.js backend, PostgreSQL with Prisma, Nightscout integration, and AI tools for both glucose pattern analysis and food recognition.

The goal is not just to collect data, but to make it more understandable, useful, and supportive in real life.

Impact

Real impact. Real lives.

For many families, especially those newly diagnosed, the hardest part of Type 1 Diabetes is not just collecting data, but understanding what it means and what to do next.

BoostT1D helps reduce uncertainty, stress, and isolation by turning confusing information into clearer guidance and by building a stronger support network around the user.
