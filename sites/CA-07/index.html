The Gameplay

Why is recycling confusing? We decided to fix it by making it a game. RecycleRightCA uses intelligent scanning to identify your waste and awards you points for making the right choice. It's an interactive quest for a cleaner California.

The Minds

We are two student developers from Franklin High School. This project was born from our shared belief that technology should be the primary tool for environmental preservation. By combining gamification with real-world recycling data, we've created a platform that empowers our community.
