Building a Greener California through Code

The Creators

We are student developers passionate about using technology to solve environmental challenges. Based in California, we've witnessed the importance of proper waste management firsthand. Our journey into coding started with a simple question: "Can we make recycling as easy as taking a photo?"

RecycleRightCA

RecycleRightCA is an AI-powered sustainability assistant designed to take the guesswork out of waste disposal. Whether you're at home, school, or on the go, our app helps users instantly identify what goes in which bin based on local California regulations.

Built using modern web technologies and a focus on accessibility, we aim to reduce landfill contamination one item at a time.
