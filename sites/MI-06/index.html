The Platform

How It Works

EcoTrack gives users the ability to monitor their carbon footprint in real time. The app integrates an AI-powered assistant called EcoBot that provides personalized sustainability advice based on each user's habits and lifestyle. Whether you're tracking daily commutes, energy usage, or food choices, EcoTrack helps quantify the environmental impact of everyday decisions.

Beyond tracking, EcoTrack features interactive Challenges and Trivia sections that make learning about sustainability engaging and fun. The Local page connects users to community events and volunteer opportunities in their area. Built by high school juniors from Northville, Michigan, EcoTrack is designed to empower people to make informed choices and prove that small actions collectively make a significant difference.
