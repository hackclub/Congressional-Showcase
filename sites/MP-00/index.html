The Platform

How It Works

IslaGo is a comprehensive mobile transit and tourism application designed to revolutionize navigation within the CNMI by providing residents and visitors in Saipan with real-time bus tracking, instant service notifications, and a dedicated "Rider Check-In" feature to minimize missed connections.

The app addresses the critical lack of reliable transit data that often hinders students, employees, and tourists. Beyond standard commuting, IslaGo promotes inclusivity through specialized Ride Assistance for the elderly and people with disabilities, featuring ADA-compliant UI settings while simultaneously boosting the local economy via a curated guide to Saipan's premier attractions and businesses.
