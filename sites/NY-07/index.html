02 — The Application

LearnIT

I built LearnIT because passive learning is a lie. You watch a video, drift off, and retain nothing. LearnIT turns any YouTube course into an active, adaptive learning session with an AI tutor that knows exactly where you are — and what you haven't figured out yet.

At its core is an AI model with full context — the transcript, my progress, my history. It responds as a domain expert, not a generic assistant. It adapts in real time and meets me at my exact level of understanding.

Two quiz systems run alongside: one checks comprehension at my current position in the video, and one deliberately targets past failures — building tests that attack my weakest points on purpose until they're not weak anymore.
