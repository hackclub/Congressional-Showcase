The Platform

How It Works

Find My Trial is a platform that can take a patient's medical chart, extract relevant information, find matching clinical trials, and screen the matches for the patient's location and preferences. It simplifies the complex process of navigating eligibility criteria that often prevents patients from accessing potentially life-changing treatments.

The system employs BERT and spaCy for the NLP backend, using the MIMIC-III and clinicaltrials.gov databases for model training and trial identification. By uploading a medical chart, patients receive a curated list of active, recruiting trials best suited to their specific conditions.
