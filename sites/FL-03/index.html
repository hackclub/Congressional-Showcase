The App

How It Works

Pickleball Coach AI pairs with an Arduino Nano 33 worn on the wrist to provide intelligent coaching for pickleball players. The device uses GPS and an accelerometer to analyze player movements in real time, offering feedback on technique while simultaneously monitoring for harmful motion patterns that could lead to injury.

Players can calibrate the system to their individual playing style, ensuring the coaching feedback is personalized and relevant. Whether you are working on your serve, your dink, or your overhead smash, the AI adapts its analysis to your unique form and provides actionable guidance to help you improve.
