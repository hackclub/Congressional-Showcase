Pulmo Lens

AI-powered pneumonia detection platform leveraging machine learning to assist in analyzing chest X-rays with greater clarity, speed, and purpose.

Explore Demo

About Me

Hi, I’m Krish Tripathy. I developed Pulmo Lens as a first-place winning project in the Congressional App Challenge. Inspired by real healthcare challenges, this project applies artificial intelligence to medical imaging in order to support faster analysis and more meaningful innovation in diagnostics.

What Pulmo Lens Does

X-ray Upload

Allows users to submit chest X-ray images for intelligent medical image analysis.

AI Prediction

Uses machine learning to classify scans as showing pneumonia or normal findings.

Confidence Output

Provides prediction confidence to give users a clearer view of model certainty.

Visual Insight

Supports interpretability through visual guidance such as highlighted areas of concern.

Project Impact & Innovation

Congressional Recognition

Pulmo Lens earned first place in the Congressional App Challenge, reflecting both computational skills and meaningful real-world application.

AI + Healthcare Integration

The project combines machine learning and medical imaging to demonstrate how artificial intelligence can contribute to smarter clinical support tools.

Real-World Problem Solving

By addressing delays in pneumonia detection, Pulmo Lens focuses on a genuine healthcare challenge where time and clarity matter.

Why Pulmo Lens Matters

Early Detection
Pneumonia can become life threatening when warning signs are missed or diagnosis is delayed. Pulmo Lens is designed to support faster recognition of concerning patterns in chest X-rays.
AI With Purpose
Pulmo Lens shows how artificial intelligence can move beyond theory and be applied to meaningful healthcare challenges, transforming machine learning into a tool with real social value.
