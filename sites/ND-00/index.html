What is WaterWise?

WaterWise aims to help farmers with irrigation by giving them a peek into the future.

Moisture Forecasts

WaterWise makes daily forecasts 7 days of soil moisture based on public weather data, eliminating guesswork and improving irrigation precision.

Localized Alerts

Users can set specific areas to be notified of future moisture events, enabling quicker response to protect the well-being of crops.

Data Visualization

Users have access to various data visualization tools, providing an effective surface for communicating the weather and moisture environment.

About forecasts

WaterWise uses an XGBoost regressor to forecast soil moisture.

Predictions are generated from 7-day ahead weather forecasts, and a context-providing 14-day window of previously observed soil moisture and weather information.
