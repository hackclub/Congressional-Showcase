The App

How It Works

Watts Up! is an e-bike aggregator that brings every nearby e-bike option into one place. Instead of switching between multiple apps like Lyft, Lime, and others, users open Watts Up! to see all available electric bikes on a single map, compare pricing and availability, and start riding in seconds.

The team was motivated by the environmental impact of short car trips in urban areas — cars are responsible for a large portion of US carbon emissions. By making e-bikes the easiest choice for short-distance travel, Watts Up! aims to reduce carbon footprints one ride at a time. Built with JavaScript, the app is designed for speed and simplicity.
