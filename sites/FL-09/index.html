Hurricane Helper

Hurricane Helper is an iOS app that help users, especially Florida residents, with hurricane preparation. The first page features a to-do checklist where the user has a simple list of essential hurricane preparation tips which they can check on or off. The app also includes a Hurricane AI Chatbot designed to answer any questions about hurricanes preparedness, resources and safety. Then, there is the alerts tab where the user can get real time weather updates, through the National Weather Service's API, for all of Florida. Lastly, there is the shelter finder page where I compiled a list of evacuation shelters throughout Florida and put it in an interactive map that links directly to Apple Maps for easy navigation.

Swift, MapKit, National Weather Service API, Landbot AI chatbot
