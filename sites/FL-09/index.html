About the App

Hurricanes are one thing, but actually preparing for one is something completely different. There is so much information out there on how to get ready, and with the stress of a hurricane, it’s hard to keep track of everything to make sure you’re truly prepared. I’ve made this app to help alleviate some of the stress that comes with hurricanes. Hurricane Helper is an iOS app that help users, especially Florida residents, with hurricane preparation.

Features

Emergency Checklist


Know exactly how to prepare with a clear list that you can easily tick off.

AI Chatbot


Trained on numerous hurricane preparedness websites.

Real Time Alerts


Stay updated with live hurricane warnings and alerts.

Shelter Map


Interactive map with hurricane shelters pinned with their locations.
