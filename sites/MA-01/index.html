Patient Advocate

This app is for anyone who has ever forgotten something at a doctor's appointment. Before the appointment, the app prepares what you are going to say, and during the appointment, it makes sure everything is asked, and it leaves you with a good understanding at the end of your appointment.

I built the app using React,Tailwind and other libraries.
