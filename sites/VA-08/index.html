The Platform

How It Works

EquiLense analyzes gender-related words, phrases, pronouns, names, and emotional descriptions, comparing the treatment of men versus women in media content. It flags biased and unfair phrases, giving users a clear picture of how language shapes narratives.

Users paste an article or connect a news feed and get visual results about gender representation, biased language, and viewpoint balance within seconds. The app is built with Swift and SwiftUI in Xcode for the frontend, powered by a Python and Flask backend that handles AI-driven NLP analysis.
